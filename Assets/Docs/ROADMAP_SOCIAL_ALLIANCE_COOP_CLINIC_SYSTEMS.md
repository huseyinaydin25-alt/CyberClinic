# Roadmap Addendum — Social, Alliance, League, and Co-op Clinic Systems

**Date:** 2026-05-29  
**Status:** Added to roadmap  
**Scope:** Expand Cyber Clinic roadmap with friends, clinic-owner social systems, alliance/legion structures, league competition, alliance quests, and cooperative high-difficulty operations.

---

## Strategic reminder

Cyber Clinic is being planned as a premium, high-budget-equivalent mobile game.

The game should not remain only a solo patient puzzle experience. It should grow into a social clinic-owner ecosystem where players compare clinics, join alliances, contribute to shared goals, compete in leagues, and cooperate on extremely difficult operations.

This layer is a major retention, prestige, social pressure, and monetization amplifier.


---

## Design rule

Social and alliance systems should be designed mock/offline-first before real online services are integrated.

This keeps systems deterministic and testable while preparing for later backend services, moderation, notifications, alliance sync, leaderboard sync, event scheduling, and co-op operation persistence.

---

## Fairness rule

Alliance, league, and co-op systems may reward commitment, cosmetics, prestige, and progression speed, but should avoid unfair pay-to-win outcomes in competitive rankings.

Preferred approach:

```text
Paying can enhance expression, cosmetics, convenience, and prestige.
Core competitive performance should remain based on strategy, consistency, contribution, and progression.
```
